# Motion One vs Framer Motion: Which is Better for Animation?

## Quick Verdict
For teams of 10-50 developers with a moderate budget, Motion One is the better choice for animation due to its superior performance and scalability. However, for smaller teams or those with limited animation needs, Framer Motion's ease of use and lower cost may be more suitable. Ultimately, the decision depends on the specific use case and requirements.

## When to Choose Motion One
* If you're a 50-person SaaS company needing to create complex, high-performance animations for your web application, Motion One's advanced features and scalability make it the better choice.
* If your team has experience with CSS and JavaScript and wants fine-grained control over animation, Motion One's steep learning curve may be worth the investment.
* If you're working on a project that requires advanced features like physics-based animations or gesture recognition, Motion One's support for these features makes it the better choice.
* If your team has a large budget and needs 24/7 priority support, Motion One's enterprise support options may be worth the cost.

## When to Choose Framer Motion
* If you're a small team or solo developer with limited animation needs, Framer Motion's free, open-source option and gentle learning curve make it an attractive choice.
* If you're working on a project with simple animation requirements, Framer Motion's basic features like tweening and easing may be sufficient.
* If your team is already familiar with React, Vue, or Angular, Framer Motion's support for these frameworks and additional plugins make it a good choice.
* If you're on a tight budget and need a cost-effective solution for basic animations, Framer Motion's free option or $19/month pro plan may be the better choice.
