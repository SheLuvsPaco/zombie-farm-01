## The Top 3 Contenders

### 1. The Overall Winner: Motion Array
- **Why it wins:** Perfect balance of features and price, with a vast library of creator assets and a user-friendly interface.
- **Best Feature:** Its ability to provide unlimited downloads of 4K and HD videos, templates, and graphics, with new content added daily.
- **Price:** $29/mo

### 2. The Budget Pick: Pexels
- **Why it wins:** Free tier is generous, with access to a large library of high-quality photos, videos, and graphics.
- **Trade-off:** Missing enterprise features, such as advanced filtering and sorting options, and limited support for commercial use.

### 3. The Power User Pick: Shutterstock
- **Why it wins:** Unlimited customization options, with a vast library of creator assets and advanced search and filtering features.
- **Best Feature:** Its ability to provide detailed metadata and keywords for each asset, making it easy to find and organize specific types of content.
- **Price:** $49/mo
