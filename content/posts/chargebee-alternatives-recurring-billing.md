## The Top 3 Contenders

### 1. The Overall Winner: Stripe Billing
- **Why it wins:** Perfect balance of features and price, with a robust set of APIs and a user-friendly interface.
- **Best Feature:** Automated revenue recognition, which reduces sync time from 15 minutes to 30 seconds and improves financial reporting accuracy.
- **Price:** $29/mo (billed annually) for the basic plan, with custom pricing available for larger businesses.

### 2. The Budget Pick: Recurly
- **Why it wins:** Free tier is generous, with support for up to 100 customers and basic recurring billing features.
- **Trade-off:** Missing enterprise features, such as advanced analytics and customization options, which may limit scalability.

### 3. The Power User Pick: Zuora
- **Why it wins:** Unlimited customization options, with a flexible data model and extensive API documentation.
- **Best Feature:** Support for complex pricing models, including tiered pricing, volume discounts, and usage-based billing.
