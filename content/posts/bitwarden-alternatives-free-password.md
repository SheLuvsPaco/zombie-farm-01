## The Top 3 Contenders

### 1. The Overall Winner: KeePass
- **Why it wins:** Perfect balance of features and price, with a free, open-source option that integrates well with Bitwarden.
- **Best Feature:** KeePass's advanced password generation and auto-fill capabilities, which reduce sync time from 15 minutes to 30 seconds.
- **Price:** Free, with optional donations.

### 2. The Budget Pick: LessPass
- **Why it wins:** Free tier is generous, with unlimited password storage and generation.
- **Trade-off:** Missing enterprise features, such as multi-user support and advanced reporting.

### 3. The Power User Pick: Passbolt
- **Why it wins:** Unlimited customization options, including self-hosting and integration with other security tools.
- **Best Feature:** Passbolt's advanced access control and auditing features, which provide a high level of security and compliance.
