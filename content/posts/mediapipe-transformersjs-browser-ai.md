# MediaPipe vs Transformers.js: Which is Better for Browser AI?

Ultimately, the choice between MediaPipe and Transformers.js depends on the specific use case and requirements of the project.

## When to Choose MediaPipe

* If you need to quickly prototype a browser AI solution and don't require advanced features, MediaPipe's gentle learning curve makes it a good choice.
