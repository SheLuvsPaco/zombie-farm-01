# Calendly vs SavvyCal: Which is Better for Scheduling?

## Quick Verdict
For small to medium-sized teams with simple scheduling needs, Calendly is a cost-effective option, while SavvyCal offers a more personalized booking experience suitable for larger teams or those requiring advanced customization. Ultimately, the choice between Calendly and SavvyCal depends on your team's specific needs, budget, and desired level of customization. If personalized booking experiences are a top priority, SavvyCal is the better choice.

## When to Choose Calendly
- If you're a small team (less than 10 users) with basic scheduling needs, Calendly's lower pricing and ease of use make it an attractive option.
- For simple, one-on-one meetings, Calendly's straightforward setup and native integrations with popular calendar apps are sufficient.
- If you're a 50-person SaaS company needing to schedule demos or sales calls, Calendly's scalability and 24/7 support ensure a reliable experience.
- For teams with limited budget (less than $100/month), Calendly's pricing model is more affordable.

## When to Choose SavvyCal
- If you're a larger team (more than 50 users) requiring advanced customization options for booking pages, SavvyCal's personalized booking experience is worth the investment.
- For teams that need to schedule complex, multi-step meetings or appointments, SavvyCal's customizable workflows and conditional logic are more suitable.
- If you're a 20-person marketing agency needing to schedule client meetings with specific time slots and buffers, SavvyCal's advanced features and support for custom booking rules are beneficial.
- For teams prioritizing a tailored booking experience with custom branding and design, SavvyCal's advanced customization options are the better choice.

## Real-World Use Case: Scheduling
Let's consider a scenario where a 20-person sales team needs to schedule meetings with potential clients. With Calendly, setup takes around 1-2 hours, and ongoing maintenance is minimal. The cost for 100 users/actions would be approximately $800-$1,200 per month. However, Calendly's limited customization options might not provide the desired level of personalization. On the other hand, SavvyCal requires 2-4 hours for setup and has a higher cost of $1,200-$2,000 per month for 100 users/actions. Nevertheless, SavvyCal's advanced customization options and personalized booking experience might be worth the extra investment. A common gotcha with both tools is the need to regularly update user permissions and access controls to ensure data security.

## Migration Considerations
If switching between Calendly and SavvyCal, be aware of the following:
- Data export/import limitations: Both tools allow data export, but importing data into the new tool may require manual formatting and mapping.
- Training time needed: Calendly requires minimal training (1-2 hours), while SavvyCal needs 2-4 hours due to its more complex features.
- Hidden costs: When migrating, consider the potential costs of reconfiguring integrations, updating workflows, and retraining team members.

## FAQ
Q: Which tool is better for large teams with complex scheduling needs?
A: SavvyCal is more suitable for large teams with complex scheduling needs due to its advanced customization options and support for custom booking rules.

Q: Can I use both Calendly and SavvyCal together?
A: Yes, you can use both tools in tandem, but it's essential to consider the potential integration complexities and data consistency issues that may arise.

Q: Which has better ROI for Scheduling?
A: Based on a 12-month projection, Calendly offers a better ROI for small to medium-sized teams with simple scheduling needs, with estimated cost savings of 15-20% compared to SavvyCal. However, for larger teams with complex scheduling needs, SavvyCal's advanced features and personalized booking experience may lead to increased revenue and customer satisfaction, potentially justifying the higher cost.
