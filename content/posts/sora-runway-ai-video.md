# Sora vs Runway: Which is Better for AI Video?

## Quick Verdict
For teams with a budget over $10,000 per year and requiring advanced AI video features, Sora is the better choice due to its integration with OpenAI and more comprehensive feature set. However, for smaller teams or startups with limited budgets, Runway's more affordable pricing model and user-friendly interface make it a more suitable option. Ultimately, the decision between Sora and Runway depends on the specific needs and constraints of your team.

## When to Choose Sora
- If you're a 50-person SaaS company needing advanced AI video features, such as object detection and tracking, Sora's integration with OpenAI makes it the better choice.
- For teams with a large budget (over $10,000 per year) and requiring customized AI video solutions, Sora's custom quote-based pricing model and high level of customization make it more suitable.
- If you have an in-house AI team and need a platform that can support complex AI video workflows, Sora's advanced features and 24/7 priority support make it the better option.
- For enterprises with over 1,000 users, Sora's scalability and support for large teams make it the more reliable choice.

## When to Choose Runway
- If you're a small startup with a limited budget (under $5,000 per year), Runway's tiered pricing model and user-friendly interface make it a more affordable and accessible option.
- For teams without in-house AI expertise, Runway's gentle learning curve and basic AI video features make it easier to get started with AI video analysis.
- If you're a 10-person marketing agency needing basic video analysis and object recognition, Runway's limited customization options and standard support are sufficient for your needs.
- For small to medium-sized businesses with up to 500 users, Runway's scalability and support make it a more suitable choice.
