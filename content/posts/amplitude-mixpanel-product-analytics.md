# Amplitude vs Mixpanel: Which is Better for Product Analytics?

## Quick Verdict
For most product teams, Amplitude is the better choice due to its more comprehensive user behavior tracking features and scalable pricing model, which can support teams of all sizes. However, Mixpanel's simplicity and ease of use make it a great option for smaller teams or those with limited analytics experience. Ultimately, the decision comes down to your team's specific needs and budget.

## When to Choose Amplitude
- If you're a 50-person SaaS company needing to track complex user behavior across multiple platforms, Amplitude's advanced features and scalable pricing make it the better choice.
- If you have a large, distributed team that requires 24/7 support and dedicated account management, Amplitude's premium support options are a major advantage.
- If you need to integrate your product analytics with other tools, such as customer support or marketing automation software, Amplitude's 50+ pre-built integrations make it a great option.
- If you're looking for a platform that can support your growth, with a pricing model that scales with your user base, Amplitude is the better choice.

## When to Choose Mixpanel
- If you're a small startup with limited analytics experience, Mixpanel's simplicity and ease of use make it a great option for getting started with product analytics.
- If you have a limited budget and need a cost-effective solution for basic user behavior tracking, Mixpanel's tiered pricing model can be more affordable.
- If you're looking for a platform with a gentler learning curve, Mixpanel's intuitive interface and limited feature set make it easier to get started.
- If you're a solo founder or small team, Mixpanel's basic features and limited support may be sufficient for your needs.
