# Frase vs Surfer SEO: Which is Better for Content Brief?

## Quick Verdict
For small to medium-sized teams with a budget under $1,000 per month, Frase is the better choice for content briefs due to its more comprehensive research features and user-friendly interface. However, larger teams with more complex content needs may prefer Surfer SEO's advanced scalability and customization options. Ultimately, the decision depends on your team's specific requirements and budget.

## When to Choose Frase
* If you're a 10-person marketing team with a budget under $500 per month, Frase's affordable pricing and easy-to-use interface make it an ideal choice.
* If you prioritize comprehensive research features, such as AI-powered research and topic modeling, Frase is the better option.
* If you're a small SaaS company needing to create content briefs for a limited number of users, Frase's simplicity and cost-effectiveness make it a great fit.
* For example, if you're a 50-person SaaS company needing to create content briefs for 10 users, Frase's $99/month plan would be a cost-effective solution.

## When to Choose Surfer SEO
* If you're a large enterprise with a complex content strategy and multiple teams, Surfer SEO's advanced scalability and customization options make it a better choice.
* If you prioritize integrations with a wide range of tools, such as HubSpot and Salesforce, Surfer SEO's extensive integration library makes it a great option.
* If you have a large budget (over $1,000 per month) and need advanced features like content auditing and keyword research, Surfer SEO is the better choice.
* For instance, if you're a 100-person marketing agency with a budget over $2,000 per month, Surfer SEO's $499/month plan would provide the necessary features and support for your team.

## Real-World Use Case: Content Brief
Both Frase and Surfer SEO can handle content briefs, but they differ in setup complexity and ongoing maintenance burden. Frase requires approximately 1-2 hours to set up, while Surfer SEO requires 2-3 hours. For 100 users, Frase costs $99/month, while Surfer SEO costs $199/month. A common gotcha with both tools is the need for ongoing content optimization and refreshes to ensure relevance and accuracy.

## Migration Considerations
If switching between Frase and Surfer SEO, be aware of the following:
* Data export/import limitations: Both tools allow data export, but Surfer SEO has more flexible import options.
* Training time needed: Frase requires 1-3 days of training, while Surfer SEO requires 3-7 days.
* Hidden costs: Surfer SEO's advanced features and customization options may incur additional costs, such as consulting fees or custom development.
