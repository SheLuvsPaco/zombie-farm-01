# Automating Customer Support: A Guide for Intercom and Zendesk
As a B2B team, integrating Intercom and Zendesk can significantly improve your customer support efficiency, reducing response times and increasing customer satisfaction. This integration can lead to a substantial return on investment (ROI) by automating tasks, enhancing collaboration, and providing a unified view of customer interactions.

## Introduction to Intercom and Zendesk Integration
The integration of Intercom and Zendesk enables businesses to leverage the strengths of both platforms. Intercom's conversational AI capabilities can be combined with Zendesk's robust ticketing system to create a seamless customer support experience.

## Feature Comparison
| Feature | Intercom Capability | Zendesk Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Conversational AI | Supports chatbots and automated messaging | Limited AI capabilities, focuses on ticketing |
| Ticketing System | Basic ticketing functionality | Comprehensive ticketing system with workflow automation |
| Customer Data | Stores customer interaction history and preferences | Maintains a detailed customer database with support history |
| Integration | Supports integration with various third-party apps | Offers a wide range of integrations with external services |

## Technical Prerequisites
To integrate Intercom and Zendesk, you will need:
- Intercom API access or webhooks
- Zendesk API access or webhooks

## The Workflow
The integration workflow can be broken down into the following 5 steps:
1. **Trigger**: When a customer initiates a conversation in Intercom, a new ticket is created in Zendesk.
2. **Data Sync**: Customer data from Intercom is synced with Zendesk to ensure a unified customer view.
3. **Assignment**: The newly created ticket in Zendesk is assigned to a support agent based on predefined rules.
4. **Resolution**: Once the issue is resolved, the ticket is updated in Zendesk, and the customer is notified through Intercom.
5. **Feedback**: The customer is asked for feedback through Intercom, which is then stored in Zendesk for future reference.

## Best Practices
When integrating Intercom and Zendesk, it's essential to prioritize data security and sync frequency. Ensure that all data exchanges are encrypted and comply with relevant regulations. Regularly review and adjust the sync frequency to avoid data duplication and ensure timely updates.

> [!TIP]
> **Pro-Tip:** To maximize the benefits of this integration, establish clear workflows and automation rules to minimize manual intervention and reduce the risk of human error.

## Frequently Asked Questions
1. **Q: Can I use Intercom's conversational AI to automate ticket routing in Zendesk?**
   A: Yes, Intercom's conversational AI can be used to automate ticket routing in Zendesk based on predefined rules and customer input.
2. **Q: How often should I sync data between Intercom and Zendesk?**
   A: The sync frequency depends on your business needs, but it's recommended to sync data at least every 15 minutes to ensure timely updates.
3. **Q: Are there any limitations to the types of data that can be synced between Intercom and Zendesk?**
   A: While most customer data can be synced, there may be limitations based on the specific plans and configurations of your Intercom and Zendesk accounts. Consult the documentation for each platform for more information.
