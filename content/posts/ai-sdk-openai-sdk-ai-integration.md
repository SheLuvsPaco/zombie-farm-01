Ultimately, the choice between AI SDK and OpenAI SDK depends on the specific use case and scalability requirements.

## When to Choose AI SDK
- If you're a 100-person enterprise software company needing to integrate multiple AI models, including computer vision and natural language processing, AI SDK offers the necessary customization and support.
- For teams with complex AI requirements, such as real-time object detection or sentiment analysis, AI SDK provides the flexibility to choose from a range of AI models.
- If your team has a large budget (over $50,000 per year) and requires priority support, AI SDK is the better choice.
- For example, if you're a 50-person SaaS company needing to integrate AI-powered chatbots and predictive analytics, AI SDK offers the necessary scalability and customization.

## When to Choose OpenAI SDK
- If you're a 10-person startup with straightforward language processing needs, such as text classification or language translation, OpenAI SDK offers a cost-effective and easy-to-use solution.
- For small teams with limited AI expertise, OpenAI SDK provides a gentle learning curve and community support.
- If your team has a limited budget (under $10,000 per year) and requires a simple AI integration, OpenAI SDK is the better choice.
- For example, if you're a 20-person marketing agency needing to integrate AI-powered content generation, OpenAI SDK offers a straightforward and affordable solution.
