# Free Airtable Social Media Calendar Template (2026 Blueprint)

## Why You Need This Template
Building a social media calendar from scratch can be a significant time waste, taking up to 5 hours to set up and customize. This template handles automated status tracking and view filtering, saving you around 2 hours per week in manual updates. This template is ideal for freelancers, small agencies, and marketing teams who need to streamline their social media content planning and tracking.

## Template Features Breakdown
- **Core View 1:** The main dashboard, titled "Social Media Calendar," provides a comprehensive overview of all scheduled posts, including the date, time, platform, content type, and status. This view allows you to quickly identify upcoming posts and track their status.
- **Automation:** The template includes pre-built automation logic that updates the status of posts from "Draft" to "Scheduled" and then to "Published" based on the scheduled date and time. This automation saves around 30 minutes per day in manual status updates.
- **Fields Included:** The template includes key data points such as:
  - Post Date and Time
  - Platform (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.)
  - Content Type (Image, Video, Link, Text)
  - Status (Draft, Scheduled, Published)
  - Engagement Metrics (Likes, Comments, Shares)

## How to Install & Customize
1. **Duplicate:** [Click here to copy to your workspace](https://airtable.com/your-workspace) to duplicate the template and start using it immediately.
2. **Setup:** Change the 'Status' column to match your workflow by adding or removing status options. For example, you can add a "Pending Approval" status to fit your team's review process.
3. **Connect:** Integrate with your Content Marketing workflow by linking the template to your content library or blog. This connection enables you to easily access and schedule pre-approved content.

## "Supercharge" This Template
> [!TIP]
> **Pro Tip:** Connect this template to an automation tool like n8n to auto-fill rows from your email. For instance, you can set up a workflow that automatically creates a new row in the template whenever you receive an email with a specific subject line or keyword.

## FAQ
Q: Is this free on the Airtable plan?
A: Yes, this template is compatible with the free Airtable plan, allowing you to use it without incurring additional costs.

Q: Can I share this with clients?
A: You can share this template with clients by granting them "Editor" or "Read-only" permissions, depending on your collaboration needs. To share, click on the "Share" button in the top-right corner of the template and enter the client's email address. You can also set up a shared view that only shows the client's specific social media channels or content.
