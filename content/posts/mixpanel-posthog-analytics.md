# Mixpanel vs PostHog: Which is Better for Analytics?

## Quick Verdict
For large enterprises with complex analytics needs, Mixpanel is the better choice due to its robust enterprise features and scalability. However, for smaller teams or those on a tighter budget, PostHog's open-source model and lower costs make it an attractive alternative. Ultimately, the decision comes down to team size, budget, and specific use case requirements.

| Data Retention | 5-year data retention | 1-year data retention (configurable) | Mixpanel |

## When to Choose Mixpanel
* If you're a 100-person enterprise software company needing advanced analytics and A/B testing capabilities, Mixpanel's robust features and scalability make it the better choice.
* If you have a large budget and require 24/7 priority support, Mixpanel's enterprise plan is the way to go.
* If you need to integrate with a wide range of third-party tools and services, Mixpanel's 50+ pre-built integrations make it a good fit.
* If you're already using other Mixpanel products, such as their customer journey mapping tool, it may be more convenient to stick with their analytics platform.

## When to Choose PostHog
* If you're a 10-person startup with limited budget and simple analytics needs, PostHog's open-source model and lower costs make it an attractive alternative.
* If you value flexibility and customization, PostHog's open-source nature allows for greater control and modification.
* If you're already familiar with open-source tools and have a strong technical team, PostHog's community-driven approach may be a good fit.
* If you need to get up and running quickly, PostHog's gentle learning curve and simple setup process make it a good choice.
