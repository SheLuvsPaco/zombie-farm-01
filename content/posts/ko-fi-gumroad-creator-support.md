## When to Choose Ko-fi

* If you're a 10-person team with a budget under $50 per month, Ko-fi's free plan and low transaction fees are a cost-effective option.
* If you're a creator who wants to focus on building a community and doesn't need complex sales and distribution features, Ko-fi's simplicity and focus on tipping and membership make it a good fit.
* For example, if you're a 20-person podcast team needing a simple way to accept tips and memberships from listeners, Ko-fi's easy setup and low fees make it a great choice.

## When to Choose Gumroad
