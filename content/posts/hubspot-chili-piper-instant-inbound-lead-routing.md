# Automating Instant Inbound Lead Routing: A Guide for HubSpot and Chili Piper
The integration of HubSpot and Chili Piper can significantly benefit B2B teams by streamlining the process of routing demo requests to the right representatives and booking meetings in real-time. This results in reduced response times, leading to higher conversion rates and improved customer satisfaction.

## Introduction
By integrating HubSpot and Chili Piper, businesses can automate the process of lead routing, ensuring that potential customers are connected with the most suitable representatives promptly. This integration can lead to increased efficiency, better lead management, and ultimately, higher sales.

## Feature Comparison
| Feature | HubSpot Capability | Chili Piper Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Lead Management | Stores and manages lead information | Routes leads to appropriate representatives |
| Meeting Scheduling | Allows for manual meeting scheduling | Automates meeting scheduling based on representative availability |
| Real-time Routing | Limited real-time routing capabilities | Routes leads in real-time to available representatives |
| Integration | Supports integration with various tools | Specifically designed for seamless integration with HubSpot |

## Technical Prerequisites
To integrate HubSpot and Chili Piper, you will need:
- HubSpot API access for lead data and workflow automation
- Chili Piper API access for meeting scheduling and routing configuration
- Webhooks for real-time data exchange between the two platforms

## The Workflow
The integration workflow can be broken down into a 5-step logic flow:
1. **Lead Submission**: A potential customer submits a demo request through a form on your website, which is captured by HubSpot.
2. **Lead Qualification**: HubSpot qualifies the lead based on predefined criteria.
3. **Real-time Routing**: Once qualified, the lead is routed in real-time to Chili Piper.
4. **Representative Assignment**: Chili Piper assigns the lead to the most suitable representative based on availability and routing rules.
5. **Meeting Scheduling**: Chili Piper automatically schedules a meeting between the lead and the assigned representative.

## Best Practices
To ensure a smooth integration, consider the following:
- **Data Security**: Ensure all data exchanged between HubSpot and Chili Piper is encrypted and compliant with relevant data protection regulations.
- **Sync Frequency**: Configure the sync frequency according to your business needs to avoid overwhelming the systems with too many requests.

> [!TIP]
> **Pro-Tip:** Regularly review and update your routing rules in Chili Piper to reflect changes in your sales team's structure or availability, ensuring leads are always routed to the most appropriate representative.

## FAQ
1. **Q: How does the integration handle lead routing when a representative is unavailable?**
   A: Chili Piper can be configured to route leads to the next available representative or to notify the sales team of the lead's interest.
2. **Q: Can the integration be customized to fit specific business needs?**
   A: Yes, both HubSpot and Chili Piper offer customization options to tailor the integration to your business requirements.
3. **Q: What kind of support does Chili Piper offer for the integration with HubSpot?**
   A: Chili Piper provides comprehensive support, including documentation, customer success teams, and community forums to help with the integration and any issues that may arise.
