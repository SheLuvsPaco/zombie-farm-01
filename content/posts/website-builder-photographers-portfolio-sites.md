## The Top 3 Contenders

### 1. The Overall Winner: Wix
- **Why it wins:** Perfect balance of features and price, with a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to create a professional-looking portfolio site.
- **Best Feature:** Wix's ADI (Artificial Design Intelligence) tool, which can create a custom website design in under 10 minutes, saving photographers around 5 hours of design time.
- **Price:** $14/mo for the Combo plan, which includes a custom domain and 3 GB of storage.

### 2. The Budget Pick: WordPress.com
- **Why it wins:** Free tier is generous, with 3 GB of storage and a custom domain, making it an excellent choice for photographers just starting out.
