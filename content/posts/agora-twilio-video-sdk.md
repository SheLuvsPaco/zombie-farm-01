# Agora vs Twilio: Which is Better for Video SDK?

## Quick Verdict
For small to medium-sized teams with limited budgets, Agora's competitive pricing and ease of use make it an attractive choice for Video SDK. However, larger enterprises with complex integration requirements may prefer Twilio's robust features and scalability. Ultimately, the decision between Agora and Twilio depends on your team's specific needs and priorities.

## When to Choose Agora
* If you're a 10-person startup with a limited budget and need a simple, easy-to-use Video SDK solution, Agora's pay-as-you-go pricing and ease of use make it an excellent choice.
* If you're a 50-person SaaS company needing to integrate video conferencing into your existing platform, Agora's pre-built integrations and competitive pricing make it a cost-effective option.
* If you're a developer looking for a straightforward, easy-to-implement Video SDK solution, Agora's documentation and support resources make it a great choice.
* If you're a small business with limited IT resources, Agora's cloud-based infrastructure and automatic software updates reduce the maintenance burden.

## When to Choose Twilio
* If you're a large enterprise with complex integration requirements and need a highly scalable Video SDK solution, Twilio's robust features and support for up to 100,000 concurrent users make it the better choice.
* If you're a company with a large, distributed team and need advanced video conferencing features like multi-party calls and screen sharing, Twilio's Video SDK features and support for up to 50 participants make it the better option.
* If you're a developer looking for a highly customizable Video SDK solution with a wide range of integrations, Twilio's API-based approach and 50+ pre-built integrations make it a great choice.
* If you're a company with a large budget and need 24/7 phone support, Twilio's premium support options make it the better choice.
