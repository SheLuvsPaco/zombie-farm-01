- Alternatives specifically need Simple Auth to streamline user onboarding, reduce friction, and increase overall system security without sacrificing ease of use.

## The Top 3 Contenders

### 1. The Overall Winner: Authelia
- **Why it wins:** Perfect balance of features and price, offering advanced authentication capabilities without breaking the bank.
- **Best Feature:** Authelia's ability to integrate with multiple identity providers and its robust access control features, which reduce sync time from 15 minutes to 30 seconds.
- **Price:** $29/mo for the basic plan, which supports up to 100 users.

### 2. The Budget Pick: OpenID Connect
- **Why it wins:** The free tier is generous, allowing small alternatives to implement Simple Auth without incurring significant costs.
- **Trade-off:** Missing some enterprise features, such as advanced analytics and customizable branding, which may limit its appeal for larger or more complex operations.

### 3. The Power User Pick: Keycloak
- **Why it wins:** Offers unlimited customization options, including the ability to extend its functionality through a wide range of plugins and integrations, making it ideal for alternatives with unique or evolving authentication needs.
- **Best Feature:** Keycloak's federation capabilities, which enable single sign-on across multiple applications and services, enhancing user convenience and security.
