## When to Choose Rytr

* If you're a solo entrepreneur or small business owner looking for an affordable AI writing solution, Rytr's premium plan offers more advanced features at a lower cost than Copy.ai.

* If you're a 50-person SaaS company with a large marketing team and a budget of $2,000/month, Copy.ai's pro plan offers advanced features, such as long-form content generation and custom templates, that can support your complex AI writing needs.

* For instance, if you're a 100-person enterprise company needing to generate high-quality, customized content at scale, Copy.ai's advanced features and support can help you achieve your goals.
