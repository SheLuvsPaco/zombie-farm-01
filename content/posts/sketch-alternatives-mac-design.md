# 5 Best Sketch Tools for Alternatives in 2026

## Why Alternatives Need Specific Tools
- Generic tools fail because they often lack the specific features and compatibility required for Mac design, resulting in inefficient workflows and compatibility issues.
- Alternatives specifically need Mac Design capabilities to cater to the unique requirements of Apple users, including compatibility with macOS and integration with other Apple devices.
- We tested these tools for Apple users, evaluating their performance, features, and pricing to provide a comprehensive comparison.

## The Top 3 Contenders

### 1. The Overall Winner: Figma
- **Why it wins:** Perfect balance of features and price, offering a wide range of tools and collaboration features at an affordable cost.
- **Best Feature:** Real-time collaboration, allowing multiple users to work on a design project simultaneously, reducing sync time from 15 minutes to 30 seconds.
- **Price:** $12/month (billed annually) for the professional plan.

### 2. The Budget Pick: Gravit Designer
- **Why it wins:** Free tier is generous, offering a wide range of features and tools, including vector editing and design templates.
- **Trade-off:** Missing enterprise features, such as advanced collaboration and security tools, making it less suitable for large-scale design projects.

### 3. The Power User Pick: Sketch
- **Why it wins:** Unlimited customization, allowing users to create custom plugins and scripts to automate repetitive tasks and streamline their workflow.
- **Best Feature:** Advanced vector editing tools, including boolean operations and path manipulation, making it ideal for complex design projects.

## Verdict: Which Should You Choose?
- **Choose Figma if:** You have a budget and want speed, collaboration, and a wide range of features.
- **Choose Gravit Designer if:** You are bootstrapping or working on small-scale projects, and need a free tool with a generous feature set.
- **Choose Sketch if:** You are a power user who requires advanced vector editing tools and unlimited customization options.
