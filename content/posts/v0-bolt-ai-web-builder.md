# v0 vs Bolt: Which is Better for AI Web Builder?

For small to medium-sized teams with a budget under $10,000 per year, v0 is a more cost-effective solution for AI web builder, offering a simpler learning curve and more straightforward pricing model. However, for larger teams or those requiring more advanced features, Bolt's scalability and extensive integration options make it a better choice. Ultimately, the decision between v0 and Bolt depends on the specific needs and constraints of your project.

## When to Choose v0

* If you're a small team with basic integration needs, v0's pre-built integrations may be sufficient.

* For instance, if you're a 100-person enterprise with multiple AI web builder projects, Bolt's scalability and support features make it a more suitable option.
