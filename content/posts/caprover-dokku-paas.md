# CapRover vs Dokku: Which is Better for PaaS?

## Quick Verdict
For small to medium-sized teams with limited budgets, CapRover is a more cost-effective option with a gentler learning curve, while larger enterprises may prefer Dokku for its extensive scalability and customization options. Ultimately, the choice between CapRover and Dokku depends on your team's specific needs and priorities. If one-click apps are a top priority, CapRover has a slight edge.

## When to Choose CapRover
- If you're a 10-person startup with a limited budget and need to quickly deploy a web application with one-click apps, CapRover is a great choice.
- For teams with limited DevOps expertise, CapRover's gentle learning curve and automated features make it an attractive option.
- If you're a 50-person SaaS company needing to deploy multiple web applications with automated SSL and load balancing, CapRover can handle the task with ease.
- For small teams with simple deployment needs, CapRover's free, open-source model and low maintenance burden make it an excellent choice.

## When to Choose Dokku
- If you're a 100-person enterprise with complex deployment needs and require extensive customization options, Dokku's scalability and flexibility make it a better fit.
- For teams with experienced DevOps engineers, Dokku's steeper learning curve and extensive plugin ecosystem can be leveraged to create customized deployment solutions.
- If you're a 20-person team with a mix of simple and complex deployment needs, Dokku's core plugins and community-driven support can help you navigate both scenarios.
- For large teams with high traffic and demanding scalability requirements, Dokku's horizontal and vertical scaling capabilities make it a more suitable choice.
