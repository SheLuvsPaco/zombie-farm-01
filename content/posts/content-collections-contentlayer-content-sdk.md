# Content Collections vs Contentlayer: Which is Better for Content SDK?

For small to medium-sized teams with limited budgets, Content Collections is a more suitable choice due to its lower pricing model and easier learning curve. However, for larger teams with more complex content needs, Contentlayer's scalability and advanced features make it a better option. Ultimately, the choice between Content Collections and Contentlayer depends on the team's specific use case and requirements.

## When to Choose Content Collections

* For example, if you're a 50-person SaaS company needing a simple content management system for your blog and knowledge base, Content Collections can be set up in under a week and costs $500/month.

## When to Choose Contentlayer

* If you're looking for a content management solution with advanced analytics and personalization features, Contentlayer is a better choice.
* For instance, if you're a 500-person e-commerce company needing a content management system that can handle large volumes of product data and provide personalized recommendations, Contentlayer's scalability and advanced features make it a better fit.
