# TinaStack vs Coolify: Which is Better for PaaS?

## Quick Verdict
For small to medium-sized teams with a budget under $10,000 per month, TinaStack is the better choice due to its more flexible pricing model and easier learning curve. However, for larger teams or enterprises with complex PaaS requirements, Coolify's advanced features and scalability make it a more suitable option. Ultimately, the choice between TinaStack and Coolify depends on your team's specific needs and use case.

## When to Choose TinaStack

* If you need to quickly deploy a small-scale PaaS solution, TinaStack's fast setup time (less than 1 hour) and minimal maintenance burden make it a good option.
* If you're a developer looking for a flexible and customizable PaaS solution, TinaStack's open-source nature and active community make it a great choice.
* For example, if you're a 50-person SaaS company needing to deploy a small-scale PaaS solution for a specific project, TinaStack's cost-effective pricing and ease of use make it a suitable option.
