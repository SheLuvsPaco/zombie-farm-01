## The Top 3 Contenders

### 1. The Overall Winner: Stripe
- **Why it wins:** Perfect balance of features and price, with a robust set of APIs and a user-friendly interface that reduces integration time by up to 50%.

### 2. The Budget Pick: Square
- **Why it wins:** Free tier is generous, with no monthly fees and a competitive processing rate of 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction.
- **Trade-off:** Missing enterprise features, such as advanced reporting and customization options, which can limit its scalability for large businesses.

### 3. The Power User Pick: Authorize.net

- **Best Feature:** Authorize.net's advanced fraud detection tools, which use machine learning algorithms to identify and prevent fraudulent transactions, reducing chargebacks by an average of 30%.
