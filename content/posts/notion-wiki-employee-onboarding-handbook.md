# Free Notion Employee Onboarding Handbook Template (2026 Blueprint)

## Why You Need This Template
Building an employee onboarding handbook from scratch can be a time-consuming process, taking up to 10 hours or more to create and customize. This template handles the heavy lifting by providing standardized new hire documentation, saving you around 8 hours of manual work. This template is ideal for small to medium-sized businesses, freelancers, and agencies looking to streamline their onboarding process.

With this template, you can ensure that all new hires receive the same level of information and training, reducing the risk of knowledge gaps and improving overall productivity. By using a standardized template, you can also reduce the time spent on onboarding by up to 30%, allowing you to focus on more critical tasks.

## Template Features Breakdown
- **Core View 1:** The main dashboard of this template is a comprehensive table that outlines all the necessary steps and information for new hires, including company policies, benefits, and job expectations. This table is customizable, allowing you to add or remove columns as needed to fit your specific workflow.
- **Automation:** This template includes pre-built logic that automatically assigns tasks and due dates to new hires, ensuring that they complete all necessary steps in a timely manner. For example, the template can automatically send reminders to new hires to complete their paperwork or attend training sessions.
- **Fields Included:** The template includes key data points such as:
  * Employee contact information
  * Job title and description
  * Company policies and procedures
  * Benefits and compensation information
  * Training and development programs
  * Performance evaluation criteria

## How to Install & Customize
1. **Duplicate:** [Click here to copy to your workspace](https://www.notion.so/Employee-Onboarding-Handbook-Template) to get started with your own customizable template.

3. **Connect:** Integrate with Notion's Wiki feature if needed, to provide new hires with access to additional company information and resources.
