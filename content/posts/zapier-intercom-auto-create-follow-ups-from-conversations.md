# Automating Auto-Create Follow-Ups from Conversations: A Guide for Zapier and Intercom
As a B2B team, integrating Zapier and Intercom can significantly improve your customer engagement and follow-up process. By automating tasks, CRM updates, and notifications, you can ensure that high-intent messages from customers do not slip through the cracks, resulting in increased conversion rates and improved customer satisfaction.

## Introduction
The integration of Zapier and Intercom offers a substantial return on investment (ROI) for B2B teams. By automating follow-ups from conversations, teams can reduce manual labor, increase response times, and enhance overall customer experience.

## Feature Comparison
| Feature | Zapier Capability | Intercom Capability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Automation | Triggers and actions for various apps | Automated workflows for customer engagement |
| Integration | Supports over 1,000 apps | Integrates with popular CRM and marketing tools |
| Customization | Allows for custom API connections | Provides customizable messaging and workflows |

## Technical Prerequisites
To integrate Zapier and Intercom, you will need:
* Zapier API access
* Intercom API access or webhooks
* A Zapier account with the Intercom integration enabled

## The Workflow
The 5-step logic flow for auto-creating follow-ups from conversations is:
1. A customer sends a high-intent message through Intercom.
2. Zapier triggers an action based on the message content or intent.
3. Intercom performs an action, such as creating a new conversation or updating a customer profile.
4. Zapier creates a new task or updates a CRM record based on the Intercom action.
5. The team receives a notification about the new task or updated CRM record.

## Best Practices
To ensure a secure and efficient integration, consider the following best practices:
* Limit API access to necessary personnel and apps.
* Set a reasonable sync frequency to avoid overwhelming your systems.
> [!TIP]
> **Pro-Tip:** Use Zapier's built-in filtering and sorting features to prioritize high-intent messages and reduce noise in your workflow.

## FAQ
1. **Q: Can I customize the triggers and actions in my Zapier-Intercom workflow?**
A: Yes, Zapier and Intercom offer customizable triggers and actions to fit your specific use case.
2. **Q: How often does Zapier sync with Intercom?**
A: Zapier syncs with Intercom at a frequency determined by your account plan and workflow settings.
3. **Q: What types of notifications can I receive from my Zapier-Intercom integration?**
A: You can receive notifications about new tasks, updated CRM records, or changes to customer profiles, depending on your workflow configuration.
