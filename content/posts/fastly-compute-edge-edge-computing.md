# Fastly Compute vs @edge: Which is Better for Edge Computing?

## Quick Verdict
For teams with a budget over $10,000 per month and requiring advanced edge computing capabilities, Fastly Compute is the better choice. However, for smaller teams or those with limited JavaScript expertise, @edge offers a more accessible and cost-effective solution. Ultimately, the decision depends on your specific use case and the trade-offs between cost, complexity, and functionality.

## When to Choose Fastly Compute
* If you're a 50-person SaaS company needing to handle over 100,000 requests per second, Fastly Compute's scalability and advanced edge computing features make it the better choice.
* For teams with extensive JavaScript expertise, Fastly Compute's customizability and support for serverless functions are major advantages.
* If your application requires complex A/B testing and real-time analytics, Fastly Compute's edge computing features provide more flexibility and control.
* For large enterprises with a budget over $50,000 per month, Fastly Compute's custom quotes and priority support ensure a high level of service and reliability.

## When to Choose @edge
* If you're a 10-person startup with limited JavaScript expertise, @edge's more accessible pricing and moderate learning curve make it a better fit.
* For teams with smaller budgets (under $5,000 per month), @edge's tiered pricing and cost-effective solution are more attractive.
* If your application requires simple edge-side includes and A/B testing, @edge's features are sufficient and easier to implement.
* For small to medium-sized teams with limited edge computing requirements, @edge's community support and paid support options provide adequate assistance.
