# Free n8n Email Parser Workflow Template (2026 Blueprint)

## Why You Need This Template
Building an email parser workflow from scratch can be a significant time waste, requiring around 10-15 hours of development time, depending on the complexity of the workflow. This template handles the automatic extraction of leads from Gmail to Google Sheets, saving you approximately 12 hours of development time. This template is ideal for freelancers, agencies, and small businesses that receive a high volume of leads via email and need to streamline their lead management process.

## Template Features Breakdown
- **Core View 1:** The main dashboard of this template features a table that displays the extracted lead information, including name, email, phone number, and message. This table is updated in real-time, allowing you to quickly review and follow up on new leads.
- **Automation:** The template includes pre-built logic that automatically extracts lead information from incoming emails in Gmail, using n8n's email parsing capabilities. This logic is triggered every 5 minutes, ensuring that new leads are extracted and added to the Google Sheet in a timely manner.
- **Fields Included:** The template extracts the following key data points from each email:
  * Name
  * Email
  * Phone number
  * Message
  * Email subject
  * Timestamp
