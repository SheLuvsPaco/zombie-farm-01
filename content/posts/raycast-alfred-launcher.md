## When to Choose Raycast
- If you're a 10-person startup with a limited budget and need a simple, AI-powered launcher, Raycast is the better choice.

- If you're a 50-person SaaS company needing to streamline workflows with AI-driven automation, Raycast's affordable pricing and AI features make it an attractive choice.

## When to Choose Alfred
- If you're a 500-person enterprise with complex workflows and require extensive customization, Alfred's scalability and customization options make it the better choice.
- If you have a large team with diverse needs and require a high level of support, Alfred's 24/7 phone and email support is a significant advantage.
- If you prioritize having a wide range of integrations and extensions, Alfred's larger library makes it a great option.
- If you're a team with advanced scripting needs and prefer manual control over automation, Alfred's scripting capabilities make it a better fit.
