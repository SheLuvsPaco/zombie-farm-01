# Canva Magic vs Leonardo.AI: Which is Better for AI Design?

## Quick Verdict
For small to medium-sized teams with a budget under $1,000 per month, Canva Magic is a more suitable choice due to its user-friendly interface and affordable pricing. However, for larger teams or enterprises with complex design requirements, Leonardo.AI's advanced features and scalability make it a better option. Ultimately, the choice between Canva Magic and Leonardo.AI depends on your team's specific needs and use case.

## When to Choose Canva Magic
* If you're a 10-person marketing team with a budget of $500 per month, Canva Magic's affordable pricing and user-friendly interface make it an ideal choice.
* If you need to create simple social media graphics or presentations, Canva Magic's basic AI-powered design tools are sufficient.
* If you're a small business with limited design experience, Canva Magic's intuitive interface and 100+ pre-made templates make it easy to get started.
* For example, if you're a 50-person SaaS company needing to create consistent branding across social media platforms, Canva Magic's template integration and affordable pricing make it a great option.

## When to Choose Leonardo.AI
* If you're a 100-person enterprise with a budget of $5,000 per month, Leonardo.AI's advanced features and scalability make it a better choice.
* If you need to create complex, custom designs with advanced AI-powered tools, Leonardo.AI's features, such as custom template integration, are essential.
* If you're a large team with experienced designers, Leonardo.AI's advanced features and 1,000+ pre-made templates make it an ideal choice.
* For instance, if you're a 200-person advertising agency needing to create high-end, custom designs for clients, Leonardo.AI's advanced AI-powered design tools and custom template integration make it the better option.
