# Automating Dev-Sales Sync: A Guide for Monday.com and Jira
As a B2B Integration Architect, I will guide you through integrating Monday.com and Jira to achieve seamless Dev-Sales Sync, resulting in increased cross-team visibility and improved collaboration.

The integration of Monday.com and Jira offers a significant return on investment (ROI) for B2B teams by streamlining communication, reducing manual data entry, and enhancing overall productivity. By automating the sync between development and sales teams, businesses can respond more quickly to customer needs, ultimately leading to higher customer satisfaction and increased revenue.

| Feature | Monday.com Capability | Jira Capability |

| Integration | Supports integration with various third-party apps | Provides integration with numerous third-party apps and services |

| Collaboration | Enables team collaboration and communication | Facilitates team collaboration and issue assignment |

## Technical Prerequisites
To integrate Monday.com and Jira, you will need:
* Monday.com API access
* Jira API access or webhooks
* A third-party integration tool or custom scripting expertise

## The Workflow
The integration workflow involves the following 5-step logic flow:
1. When a new issue is created in Jira, Monday.com is triggered to create a corresponding item.
2. Monday.com updates the item with relevant sales information, such as customer details and sales status.
3. Jira is updated with the latest sales information from Monday.com.
4. When the development team updates the issue status in Jira, Monday.com is triggered to update the corresponding item.
5. Monday.com notifies the sales team of the updated issue status, ensuring they are informed and up-to-date.

## Best Practices
To ensure a secure and efficient integration, consider the following best practices:
* Implement data encryption and access controls to protect sensitive information.
* Schedule regular syncs to maintain data consistency between Monday.com and Jira.
> [!TIP]
> **Pro-Tip:** Use a third-party integration tool to simplify the integration process and reduce the need for custom scripting.
