# Free Make Instagram Auto-Poster Template (2026 Blueprint)

## Why You Need This Template
Building an Instagram auto-poster from scratch can be a significant time waste, requiring up to 10 hours of development and testing. This template handles the scheduling and publishing of content automatically, saving you around 5 hours per week. This template is ideal for freelancers, social media managers, and agencies who need to manage multiple Instagram accounts and want to streamline their content publishing process.

## Template Features Breakdown
- **Core View 1:** The main dashboard of this template features a table with columns for post content, scheduling date and time, and publication status. This allows you to easily view and manage all your scheduled posts in one place.
- **Automation:** The template includes pre-built logic to automatically publish posts at the scheduled time, eliminating the need for manual intervention. It also handles errors and retries failed posts, ensuring that your content is published reliably.
- **Fields Included:** The template includes key data points such as post caption, image or video link, hashtags, and tagging information. It also tracks the publication status of each post, allowing you to monitor the success of your content publishing process.

## How to Install & Customize
1. **Duplicate:** [Click here to copy to your workspace](https://www.make.com/en/templates) to create a copy of the template in your Make account.
2. **Setup:** Change the 'Status' column to match your workflow, such as "Draft", "Scheduled", or "Published", to fit your specific needs.
3. **Connect:** Integrate with your Instagram account through Make's built-in Instagram module, which supports both personal and business accounts.

## "Supercharge" This Template
> [!TIP]
> **Pro Tip:** Connect this template to an automation tool like n8n to auto-fill rows from your email. For example, you can set up a workflow that extracts post content from emails sent to a specific address and automatically adds them to the template's table, streamlining your content creation process.
