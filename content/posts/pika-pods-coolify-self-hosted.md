# Pika Pods vs Coolify: Which is Better for Self-hosted?

For small to medium-sized teams with a budget under $10,000 per year, Pika Pods is a better choice due to its more affordable pricing model and easier learning curve. Ultimately, the decision between Pika Pods and Coolify depends on the specific needs and use case of the team.

## When to Choose Pika Pods

- If you're a 20-person team with a budget of $5,000 per year, Pika Pods offers a more affordable pricing model and sufficient features for small-scale self-hosted deployments.
