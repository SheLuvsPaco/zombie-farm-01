# Lemlist vs Apollo.io: Which is Better for Cold Outreach?

## Quick Verdict
For small to medium-sized teams with a budget under $1,000 per month, Lemlist is a better choice due to its more affordable pricing and easier learning curve. However, for larger teams or enterprises with more complex cold outreach needs, Apollo.io's scalability and advanced features make it a more suitable option. Ultimately, the choice between Lemlist and Apollo.io depends on your team's specific needs and budget.

## When to Choose Lemlist
* If you're a 10-person startup with a limited budget and simple cold outreach needs, Lemlist's affordable pricing and ease of use make it a great choice.
* If you prioritize ease of use and don't need advanced automation features, Lemlist's intuitive interface and quick setup process make it a good option.
* If you're already using other tools in the Lemlist ecosystem, such as Mailchimp or Hubspot, Lemlist's native integrations make it a convenient choice.
* For example, if you're a 50-person SaaS company needing to send 1,000 cold emails per month, Lemlist's $29 per user/month pricing and easy setup process make it a cost-effective option.

## When to Choose Apollo.io
* If you're a 100-person enterprise with complex cold outreach needs and a large budget, Apollo.io's advanced features and scalability make it a better choice.
* If you prioritize email deliverability and need advanced features like spam filter testing, Apollo.io's robust deliverability features make it a good option.
* If you need advanced automation features with AI-powered suggestions, Apollo.io's automation capabilities make it a suitable choice.
* For instance, if you're a 500-person sales team sending 10,000 cold emails per month, Apollo.io's $99 per user/month pricing and advanced features make it a worthwhile investment.
