# Pingdom vs UptimeRobot: Which is Better for Uptime?

## Quick Verdict
For small to medium-sized teams with a budget under $100/month, UptimeRobot is a more cost-effective solution for uptime monitoring. However, larger teams with more complex infrastructure may prefer Pingdom's advanced features and scalability. Ultimately, the choice between Pingdom and UptimeRobot depends on your specific use case and priorities.

## When to Choose Pingdom
* If you're a 50-person SaaS company needing advanced uptime reporting and customizable alerts, Pingdom is a better choice due to its scalability and feature set.
* For teams with complex infrastructure, Pingdom's advanced features and integrations with tools like PagerDuty make it a more suitable option.
* If you prioritize 24/7 phone support and a high level of customization, Pingdom is the better choice, despite its steeper learning curve.
* For large enterprises with a budget over $500/month, Pingdom's advanced features and scalability make it a more cost-effective solution in the long run.

## When to Choose UptimeRobot
* If you're a small startup or solo developer with a limited budget (under $10/month), UptimeRobot is a more affordable and easy-to-use solution for basic uptime monitoring.
* For teams with simple infrastructure and basic uptime monitoring needs, UptimeRobot's gentle learning curve and lower cost make it a more suitable option.
* If you prioritize ease of use and a simple, intuitive interface, UptimeRobot is the better choice, with a setup time of under 1 hour.
* For small to medium-sized teams with a budget under $100/month, UptimeRobot is a more cost-effective solution for basic uptime monitoring.
