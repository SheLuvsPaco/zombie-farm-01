# 5 Best Bravo Tools for Alternatives in 2026

## Why Alternatives Need Specific Tools
- Generic tools fail because they lack the specific design-to-code capabilities that alternatives require to efficiently convert Figma designs into functional apps.
- Alternatives specifically need Figma to App tools to streamline their development process, reduce manual coding, and improve collaboration between designers and developers.
- We tested these tools for their design-to-code capabilities, evaluating factors such as ease of use, customization options, and integration with popular design and development platforms.

## The Top 3 Contenders

### 1. The Overall Winner: Adobe XD
- **Why it wins:** Perfect balance of features and price, with a user-friendly interface and robust design-to-code capabilities.
- **Best Feature:** Adobe XD's "Design to Code" feature, which allows users to generate clean, production-ready code from their designs, reducing sync time from 15 minutes to 30 seconds.
- **Price:** $9.99/mo (basic plan) to $79.49/mo (business plan)

### 2. The Budget Pick: Framer
- **Why it wins:** Free tier is generous, with unlimited projects and collaborators, making it an excellent choice for startups and small teams.
- **Trade-off:** Missing enterprise features, such as advanced security and single sign-on, which may be a limitation for larger organizations.

### 3. The Power User Pick: InVision
- **Why it wins:** Unlimited customization options, with a wide range of plugins and integrations available, making it a great choice for power users who need advanced features and flexibility.
- **Best Feature:** InVision's "Design System Manager", which allows users to create and manage reusable design components, reducing design inconsistencies and improving collaboration.
