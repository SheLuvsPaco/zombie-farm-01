# Heroku Private Spaces vs Heroku: Which is Better for PaaS?

## Quick Verdict
For large enterprises with complex security and compliance requirements, Heroku Private Spaces is the better choice, offering enhanced security, isolation, and control. However, for smaller teams or those with limited budgets, Heroku's standard offering provides a more cost-effective and straightforward Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) solution. Ultimately, the decision depends on your team's specific needs, size, and budget.

## When to Choose Heroku Private Spaces
* If you're a 50-person SaaS company needing enhanced security and compliance features, such as HIPAA or PCI-DSS, Heroku Private Spaces provides the necessary controls and isolation.
* For large enterprises with complex network architectures, Heroku Private Spaces offers dedicated resources and private networking capabilities.
* If your team requires priority support and dedicated account management, Heroku Private Spaces is the better choice.
* When you need to integrate with enterprise-grade services, such as Salesforce or SAP, Heroku Private Spaces provides the necessary add-ons and support.

## When to Choose Heroku
* If you're a small team or startup with limited budget, Heroku's standard offering provides a cost-effective and straightforward PaaS solution.
* For development teams with simple deployment needs, Heroku's automated scaling and rolling updates features make it an attractive choice.
* If your team is already familiar with Heroku's ecosystem and tools, sticking with the standard offering can simplify the learning curve and reduce costs.
* When you need to quickly prototype and deploy a new application, Heroku's gentle learning curve and extensive documentation make it an ideal choice.

## Real-World Use Case: PaaS
Let's consider a 100-user SaaS application with 500 actions per day. Heroku Private Spaces would require:
* Setup complexity: 2-3 days, including configuring private networking and IPsec VPN.

In contrast, Heroku's standard offering would require:

Common gotchas include managing shared resources and scaling limitations.

## Migration Considerations
If switching between Heroku Private Spaces and Heroku:
* Data export/import limitations: Heroku provides a data export tool, but it may not work seamlessly with all add-ons.
* Training time needed: 1-2 weeks, depending on the complexity of the application and the team's familiarity with Heroku's ecosystem.
* Hidden costs: Additional costs may arise from reconfiguring add-ons, retraining staff, or rearchitecting the application.
