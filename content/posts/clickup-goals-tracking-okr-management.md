# Does ClickUp Have Goals Tracking? (2026 Update)

## The Short Answer: 
Yes with hierarchy. ClickUp's Goals Tracking feature allows users to set and manage objectives and key results (OKRs) in a hierarchical structure, enabling teams to align their efforts and track progress towards common goals. This feature provides a robust framework for OKR management, enabling users to create goals, break them down into smaller objectives, and assign tasks to team members.

## How to Use Goals Tracking in ClickUp (If Yes)
1. Navigate to the "Goals" section in your ClickUp workspace, where you can create new goals and objectives.
2. Click the "New Goal" button to set up a new objective, and then use the hierarchy feature to break it down into smaller, actionable tasks and key results.
3. Result: Your goals and objectives will be displayed in a visual hierarchy, allowing you to track progress, assign tasks, and align your team's efforts towards achieving the desired outcomes, with the ability to drill down into specific details and metrics.

## Workarounds (If No)
