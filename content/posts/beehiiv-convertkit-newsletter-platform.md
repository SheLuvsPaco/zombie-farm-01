# Beehiiv vs ConvertKit: Which is Better for Newsletter Platform?

## Quick Verdict
For small to medium-sized teams with a budget under $100/month, Beehiiv is a more cost-effective option for basic newsletter platforms. However, for larger teams or those requiring advanced automation features, ConvertKit's scalability and support make it a better choice. Ultimately, the decision depends on your team's specific needs and growth stage.

## When to Choose Beehiiv
* If you're a 10-person startup with a limited budget and basic newsletter needs, Beehiiv's affordable pricing and easy-to-use interface make it a great choice.
* If you're looking for a simple, no-frills newsletter platform with minimal setup time (less than 1 hour), Beehiiv is a good option.
* If you have a small team (less than 20 people) and don't require advanced automation features, Beehiiv's basic automation workflows may be sufficient.
* For example, if you're a 20-person e-commerce company needing a basic newsletter platform to send monthly promotions, Beehiiv's $25/month plan can save you $4/month compared to ConvertKit.

## When to Choose ConvertKit
* If you're a 50-person SaaS company with a growing subscriber list (over 1,000 subs) and need advanced automation features, ConvertKit's scalability and support make it a better choice.
* If you require a high level of customization and flexibility in your newsletter platform, ConvertKit's advanced automation workflows and 50+ native integrations provide more options.
* If you have a large team (over 50 people) and need multiple user accounts with different permission levels, ConvertKit's support for multiple user accounts and roles is more suitable.
* For instance, if you're a 100-person marketing agency with multiple clients and need a robust newsletter platform with advanced automation features, ConvertKit's $49/month plan provides more value.

## Real-World Use Case: Newsletter Platform
Let's say you're a 20-person e-commerce company with a growing subscriber list (over 1,000 subs) and need to send monthly newsletters with personalized product recommendations. Here's how each tool handles this scenario:
* Setup complexity: Beehiiv (1-2 hours), ConvertKit (2-4 hours)
* Ongoing maintenance burden: Beehiiv (1-2 hours/week), ConvertKit (2-4 hours/week)
* Cost breakdown for 100 users/actions: Beehiiv ($25/month), ConvertKit ($29/month)
* Common gotchas: Beehiiv's basic automation workflows may not be sufficient for complex personalization, while ConvertKit's advanced automation workflows require more setup time.

## Migration Considerations
If switching between these tools:
* Data export/import limitations: Both Beehiiv and ConvertKit support CSV exports, but ConvertKit has more flexible import options.
* Training time needed: 2-4 hours for Beehiiv, 4-6 hours for ConvertKit
* Hidden costs: ConvertKit's advanced automation features may require additional setup time and costs, while Beehiiv's limited scalability may require upgrading to a more expensive plan.
