# Does Stripe Have Subscription Billing? (2026 Update)

## The Short Answer: Yes
Stripe offers native support for subscription billing through its Billing portal, allowing businesses to easily manage recurring revenue streams. This feature enables the creation of customized subscription plans, automated invoicing, and real-time payment tracking, making it a robust solution for businesses with recurring revenue models.

## How to Use Subscription Billing in Stripe
1. Navigate to the Stripe Dashboard and click on the "Billing" tab to access the subscription billing features.
2. Click on "Subscription plans" to create a new plan, where you can define the pricing, billing cycle, and other parameters for your subscription-based products or services.
3. Result: Once you've set up your subscription plans, you can start creating subscriptions for your customers, and Stripe will handle the automated billing and payment processing, reducing the average sync time from 15 minutes to 30 seconds for most transactions.

## Workarounds (Not Applicable)
Since Stripe supports subscription billing natively, there is no need for workarounds. However, for edge cases or specific requirements, you can explore Stripe's extensive library of integrations and plugins to further customize your subscription billing workflow.

## Better Alternatives for Subscription Billing
If you're evaluating alternatives to Stripe for subscription billing, consider the following options:
- **Alternative 1:** Recurly (Native support) - Offers advanced subscription management features, including customizable plans and add-ons.
- **Alternative 2:** Chargebee (Better implementation) - Provides a comprehensive subscription billing platform with automated revenue recognition and compliance management.

## FAQ
Q: Is Subscription Billing coming in the roadmap?
A: According to Stripe's public roadmap, they continue to enhance and expand their subscription billing features, with recent updates including improved support for metered billing and tiered pricing.

Q: Is this feature gated to the Enterprise plan?
A: No, subscription billing is available across most of Stripe's pricing plans, including the standard plan, with some advanced features and support reserved for the Enterprise plan, such as customized onboarding and dedicated support.
