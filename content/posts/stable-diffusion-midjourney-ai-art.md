# Stable Diffusion vs Midjourney: Which is Better for AI Art?

## Quick Verdict
For small teams or individuals with limited budgets, Stable Diffusion is the better choice due to its free pricing model and robust feature set. However, for larger teams or enterprises with more complex AI art needs, Midjourney's paid subscription offers more advanced features, better support, and greater scalability. Ultimately, the choice between Stable Diffusion and Midjourney depends on your team size, budget, and specific use case.
