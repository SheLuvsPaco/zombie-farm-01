## The Top 3 Contenders

### 1. The Overall Winner: Render
- **Why it wins:** Perfect balance of features and price, offering a free tier with generous limits and paid plans starting at $7/mo.
- **Best Feature:** Automated deployment and rollback capabilities, reducing sync time from 15 minutes to 30 seconds.
- **Price:** $7/mo (paid plan)

### 2. The Budget Pick: Vercel
- **Why it wins:** Free tier is generous, with unlimited deployments and 50GB of bandwidth, making it an excellent choice for starters and small projects.
- **Trade-off:** Missing enterprise features, such as advanced security and customization options, which may be a limitation for larger or more complex projects.

### 3. The Power User Pick: Netlify
- **Why it wins:** Unlimited customization options, including support for custom domains, SSL certificates, and webhooks, making it an ideal choice for power users and large-scale applications.
- **Best Feature:** Advanced security features, including DDoS protection and automated backups, which provide an additional layer of protection and reliability.
