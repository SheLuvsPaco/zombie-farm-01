### 1. The Overall Winner: GitLab

### 2. The Budget Pick: Gitea

### 3. The Power User Pick: GitHub Enterprise
- **Why it wins:** Unlimited customization, with support for custom workflows and integrations.
- **Best Feature:** Advanced security features, including SAML and two-factor authentication.
