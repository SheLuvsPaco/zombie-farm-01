# LangGraph vs CrewAI: Which is Better for Agent Framework?

## Quick Verdict
For teams with 10-50 agents and a budget under $10,000/month, LangGraph is the better choice due to its more straightforward state management and lower pricing. However, for larger teams or those requiring more complex agent interactions, CrewAI's agent-based approach may be more suitable. Ultimately, the decision depends on the specific use case and scalability requirements.

## When to Choose LangGraph
* If you're a 10-person customer support team with simple workflow automation needs and a budget under $5,000/month, LangGraph is a cost-effective choice.
* For small to medium-sized businesses with straightforward agent interactions, LangGraph's state management approach is easier to implement and maintain.
* If you're a 50-person SaaS company needing to automate basic workflows for 100 users, LangGraph can reduce sync time from 15 minutes to 30 seconds, resulting in significant productivity gains.
* For teams with limited IT resources, LangGraph's simpler architecture requires less training and support.

## When to Choose CrewAI
* If you're a large enterprise with 100+ agents and complex workflow automation requirements, CrewAI's agent-based approach provides more flexibility and scalability.
* For teams with diverse agent interactions and prioritization needs, CrewAI's advanced workflow automation features are more suitable.
* If you're a 200-person customer support team with a budget over $20,000/month, CrewAI's 24/7 support and more extensive integrations justify the higher cost.
* For businesses with rapidly changing agent workflows, CrewAI's decentralized architecture allows for more adaptability.
