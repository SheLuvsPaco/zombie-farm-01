# 5 Best ExpressVPN Alternatives for Fast VPN in 2026

## Why Alternatives Need Specific Tools
- Generic tools fail because they often compromise on speed to provide a broad range of features, which may not be necessary for every user.
- Alternatives specifically need Fast VPN to ensure that their online activities are not hindered by slow connection speeds, which can be particularly problematic for streaming, online gaming, and large file transfers.
- We tested these alternatives for Speed optimized performance, focusing on their ability to maintain high speeds across different servers and locations.

## The Top 3 Contenders

### 1. The Overall Winner: NordVPN
- **Why it wins:** Perfect balance of features and price, offering a comprehensive set of tools including strong encryption, a no-logs policy, and a large server network.
- **Best Feature:** NordVPN's proprietary NordLynx protocol, which reduces latency and increases speeds, making it ideal for activities that require low ping times.
- **Price:** $11.95/mo, with discounts available for longer-term commitments.

### 2. The Budget Pick: ProtonVPN
- **Why it wins:** Free tier is generous, offering unlimited data and decent speeds, making it an excellent choice for those on a tight budget or who only need a VPN occasionally.
- **Trade-off:** Missing enterprise features, such as dedicated IP addresses and advanced security audits, which may be necessary for businesses or power users.

### 3. The Power User Pick: Surfshark
- **Why it wins:** Unlimited customization options, including the ability to use multiple VPN connections simultaneously and a robust set of security features like CleanWeb and MultiHop.
- **Best Feature:** Surfshark's ability to connect an unlimited number of devices with a single subscription, making it perfect for large families or teams.

- **Choose NordVPN if:** You have a budget and want a fast, reliable VPN with a strong focus on security and a user-friendly interface.
