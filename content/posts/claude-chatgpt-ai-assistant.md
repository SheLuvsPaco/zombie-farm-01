Ultimately, the choice between Claude and ChatGPT depends on your specific use case and budget.

## When to Choose Claude
* If you're a 10-person marketing team needing to generate social media posts and blog articles, Claude's artifact creation capabilities and affordable pricing make it a great choice.
* If you're a 20-person sales team looking to automate email follow-ups and lead generation, Claude's ease of use and native integrations with CRM software make it a good fit.

## When to Choose ChatGPT

* If you prioritize conversational AI and have a budget of $2,000-5,000 per month, ChatGPT is the better choice.
