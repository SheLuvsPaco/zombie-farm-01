# Metrical vs Plausible: Which is Better for Web Analytics?

For small to medium-sized teams with limited budgets, Plausible is a more cost-effective option with a simpler setup process. However, for larger teams requiring advanced features and customization, Metrical is a better choice. Ultimately, the decision between Metrical and Plausible depends on your team's specific needs and priorities.

## When to Choose Metrical

* For teams with complex analytics requirements, such as tracking multiple websites or integrating with CRM systems, Metrical's customization options make it a better fit.
* If you have a large budget and require 24/7 support, Metrical's enterprise plan is a good option.
* For example, if you're a 100-person e-commerce company with a large marketing team, Metrical's advanced features and support would be beneficial.

## When to Choose Plausible

* If you're a non-profit organization or a small business with limited resources, Plausible's flat rate pricing and limited features make it a more affordable option.
* For instance, if you're a 10-person startup with a limited marketing budget, Plausible's simplicity and cost-effectiveness would be beneficial.
