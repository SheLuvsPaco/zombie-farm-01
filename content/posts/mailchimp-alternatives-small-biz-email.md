## The Top 3 Contenders

### 1. The Overall Winner: Sendinblue
- **Why it wins:** Perfect balance of features and price, with a user-friendly interface and a wide range of templates and design options.
- **Best Feature:** Automated workflow editor, which allows users to create complex email sequences with ease.
- **Price:** $25/mo for up to 2,500 subscribers and 10,000 emails per day.

### 2. The Budget Pick: MailerLite
- **Why it wins:** Free tier is generous, with up to 1,000 subscribers and 12,000 emails per month.
- **Trade-off:** Missing enterprise features, such as advanced automation and customization options.

### 3. The Power User Pick: Klaviyo
- **Why it wins:** Unlimited customization options, with a wide range of integrations and APIs available.
- **Best Feature:** Advanced segmentation and targeting capabilities, which allow users to create highly personalized email campaigns.
- **Price:** $25/mo for up to 1,000 subscribers and 5,000 emails per day.
