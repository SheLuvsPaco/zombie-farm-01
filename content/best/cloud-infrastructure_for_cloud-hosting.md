When evaluating cloud infrastructure for cloud hosting, consider:

1. **Integration Capabilities** - Does it work with your existing tools?
2. **Scalability** - Can it grow with your team?
3. **Learning Curve** - How quickly can your team adopt it?
4. **Support Quality** - What help is available when you need it?
5. **Total Cost** - Consider all fees, not just base price

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the most popular cloud infrastructure for cloud hosting?

Based on our analysis of 20 comparisons, **API** is currently the most popular choice for cloud hosting, offering the best balance of features and pricing.

### Which cloud infrastructure has the best free tier?
