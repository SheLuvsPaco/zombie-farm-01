## Software Migration Guides

**Switch platforms without losing your data.** Our migration guides provide complete checklists for moving between popular tools.

### What You'll Find in Each Guide

- **Pre-Migration Checklist** - What to backup and document
- **Data Export** - How to get your data out
- **Data Import** - How to bring it into the new tool
- **Verification Steps** - Confirm everything transferred
- **Post-Migration Cleanup** - Cancel old subscriptions safely

### Migration Best Practices

1. **Export Everything First** - Always backup before you start
2. **Test with Sample Data** - Don't migrate production data first
3. **Keep Old Account Active** - Maintain access during transition
4. **Document Custom Configurations** - Screenshots and notes
5. **Plan for Downtime** - Schedule migrations during low-activity periods
